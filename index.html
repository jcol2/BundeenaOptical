About Ian and Bundeena Optical

Ian is a qualified Optical Dispenser/Mechanic, and has worked in the Optical Industry for over 30 years. In that time, Ian has worked in independent Optometry practices - but mainly in the wholesale area. Ian has travelled NSW, representing a number of optical wholesale companies that carry both frame & lens products. Ian developed an interest in optical lenses, and worked in this specialised field in varying capacities for 15 years with a German lens manufacturer, Rodenstock.

Ian started Bundeena Optical in mid 2017, with the primary intention of being able to provide an optical service to the local residents of Bundeena. Whether by ferry or road, anywhere out of town is fair distance. Services Bundeena Optical offers are lenses & frames, sunglasses, contact lenses, accessories, emergency repairs, after hours appointments & local home visits. Bundeena Optical has both EFTPOS & HiCaps facilities on-site, so your health fund claim may be processed on the spot. Bundeena Optical uses German made Rodenstock lenses as a preference, but also uses independent Australian laboratories.
